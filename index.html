Rengoku Kyojuro

About Rengoku Kyojuro

Rengoku Kyojuro was a major supporting character of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba and a major character in the Mugen Train Arc. He was a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps and the late Flame Hashira.

The Former Flame Hashira

Rengoku's Characteristics

  1. He's part of the Demon Slayer Corps
  2. He's the Flame Hashira
  3. His breathing style is Flame Breathing
  4. He is super cheerful, eccentric, and is pure of heart
